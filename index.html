ABOUT US

From sawmill
to the finished brush

The DREWMAX company has been operating in the brush industry since 1991. For over three decades, we have consistently developed our own production base — today, we are one of the few manufacturers in Poland who control the entire manufacturing cycle in our own plants.

We have our own sawmill, carpentry workshop, modern vacuum drying kilns, and varnishing shop. Selected beechwood comes from certified forests and enters production only after a multi-stage quality control. We source natural and synthetic bristles from trusted, long-term suppliers.

We produce a wide range of brushes — from consumer goods to specialized industrial solutions. At the customer's request, we carry out production under a private label and custom orders according to supplied specifications.

Production & Distribution

The production facility is located in Braniewo (Warmińsko-Mazurskie Province). We have our own sawmill, carpentry workshop, vacuum drying kilns, and varnishing shop — full control of the process from raw material to the final product. Distribution covers the entire country; we provide flexible delivery schedules and individual commercial terms to regular B2B partners.

Certificates & Quality

Wooden raw material comes from forests certified according to FSC standards. Products undergo quality control at every stage of production. We constantly monitor wood parameters (moisture, density, grain uniformity) and the quality of the brush bristle used.

B2B Zone

We accept wholesale orders without a minimum threshold value under steady cooperation. We offer private label production — labels, packaging, and designs according to the client's project. Please contact us to establish long-term cooperation terms.
