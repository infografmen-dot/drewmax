Our Services

FSC Beechwood

Every piece of wood that goes into production comes from certified forests and undergoes rigorous selection — we dry it in our own drying rooms to ensure durability and resistance to moisture.

100% Polish Production

From the sawmill and carpentry to the lacquer shop and bristle insertion — the entire production cycle takes place on-site. Thanks to this, we have full control over the quality of every element and short turnaround times.

Delivery Across Poland

We fulfill wholesale and retail orders with delivery throughout the country. For B2B clients, we offer individual pricing terms, flexible payment deadlines, and the possibility of production under own brand.
