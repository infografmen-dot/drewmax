COMPETITIVE WARMIA & MASURIA ECONOMY

Innovative Machinery Modernization Scheme

The DREWMAX company has implemented a comprehensive modernization plan of its carpentry and brush tufting plants located in Pieniężno. The project consisted in the acquisition of advanced computerized CNC processing centers and automated high-speed tuft insertion systems co-financed by the European Regional Development Fund.

By adopting automated manufacturing procedures, our facility achieved significant savings in raw timber trimming waste, decreased daily electrical grid loads, and significantly raised custom machining tolerances for B2B Private Label partnerships.

Project Title Modernization of DREWMAX machinery park through the acquisition of advanced automated wood machining centers and energy-efficient vacuum kilns.
Program Scheme Regional Operational Programme of the Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship for the years 2014-2020. Priority Axis 1: "Competitive Economy of Warmia and Masuria".
Project Objectives To increase production capacity, introduce new catalog brush series to European markets, reduce technological waste, and transition sawmill processes to sustainable closed-loop parameters.
Major Milestones Accomplished
  • Setting up automated CNC processing center for milling beech bases.
  • Purchasing energy-optimized vacuum drying rooms with computer humidity metrics.
  • Upgrading automatic dual-head high frequency tufting machines for precise bristle insertion.
Total Investment Value 1,200,000.00 PLN
European Union Contribution 650,000.00 PLN
