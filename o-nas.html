Our Tradition & Story

SINCE 1991

35 Years of Brush Craftsmanship

The story of DREWMAX began in 1991 in Warmia and Masuria. Operating initially as a local woodturning workshop, we spent over three decades consistently expanding our technology and expertise. Today, we are proud to stand as a premier Polish manufacturer controlling 100% of the manufacturing chain under one management.

By controlling every stage from sawmill logs slicing to fiber tufting and brand packaging, we eliminate external logistical delays and retain full mastery of wood moisture, visual grade selection and bristle density. This unified control ensures stable wholesale terms and reliable lead times for B2B partners worldwide.

MANUFACTURING CYCLE

From the Log to the Finished Brush

We operate our own sawmill, computerized woodworking carpentry, vacuum kiln dryer, and varnishing systems.

01

Local Sawmill

We source selected round beechwood directly from certified state forests, slicing and preparing raw brush blanks locally.

02

Carpentry

Precision CNC millers and lathes shape, bevel, chamfer and drill clean mounting holes into pristine beechwood blocks.

03

Kiln Drying

Our proprietary vacuum drying kilns bring moisture down to a stable 8-12%, ensuring zero splitting, warping or rotting.

04

Varnishing

Specialized drum and spray painting lines apply protective lacquers, wood stains, or custom owner branding logo prints.

05

Bristle Tufting

Automatic high-speed tuft insertion machines securely lock premium natural horsehair, synthetic PET or steel filaments.

CERTIFIED QUALITY

FSC Beech Wood Selection

At DREWMAX, sustainability is not an afterthought. Every piece of raw beech wood used in our factory comes from responsibly managed forests certified in accordance with FSC standards.

Our quality control inspects grain density, removes core cracks and ensures uniform structure before raw feedstock enters our drying kilns. This thorough attention yields robust brush blocks that resist daily friction stress, dropping chips, and heavy moisture expansion.
