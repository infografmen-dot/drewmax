Our Tradition & Story

SINCE 2011

DREWMAX – tradition, quality
and reliability since 2011

Since 2011, the DREWMAX Production and Trade Company has specialized in the manufacture and sale of high-quality wooden and plastic brushes designed for household, street, and garden cleaning, as well as scrubbing brushes. Our product range also includes small wooden household utensils, including wooden spoons, forks, and cutting boards.

Thanks to our extensive experience, attention to detail, and personalized approach to each customer, we have earned the trust of numerous clients both in Poland and abroad. We focus on reliability, competitive pricing, and timely order fulfillment. We continuously expand our offer to meet market expectations and deliver durable, functional, and aesthetically pleasing products.

DREWMAX is a proven partner for whom quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction are of paramount importance.

CERTIFIED QUALITY

FSC Beech Wood Selection

DREWMAX uses beechwood sourced from legal and responsibly managed forests with FSC certification. Caring for the natural environment and high quality standards are our priority. Beechwood stands out for its high quality, stability, and durability, thanks to which it retains its functional properties and resistance to long-term use.
