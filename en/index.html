ABOUT US

DREWMAX – tradition, quality
and reliability
since 2011

Since 2011, the DREWMAX Production and Trading Company has specialized in the manufacture and sale of high-quality wooden and plastic brushes designed for cleaning homes, streets, and gardens, as well as scrubbing brushes. Our offer also includes a selection of wooden household utility items, such as wooden spoons, forks, and cutting boards.

Thanks to our extensive experience, attention to craftsmanship, and an individual approach to each customer, we have gained the trust of many clients both domestically and abroad. We focus on reliability, competitive pricing, and timely order fulfillment. We continuously expand our range to meet market demands and deliver products that are durable, functional, and aesthetically pleasing.

DREWMAX is a proven partner for whom quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction are paramount.

Polish manufacturer of wooden and plastic brushes and turned wood components. Over 30 years of family craftsmanship and premium FSC beechwood quality.
